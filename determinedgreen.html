Determined Green

This project explored an app design for an energy company that would allow its clients to learn more about their energy usage and encourage them to make sustainable and energy conscious decisions.

Determined Green is an app designed to help users understand and minimize their household's impact on climate change. It provides features such as calculating the carbon footprint of energy use and providing community challenges. Additionally, the app allows users to monitor their electricity usage, enabling them to identify behavior patterns and discover ways to save money while reducing their environmental impact.

The app focuses on facilitating goal-setting for users, making it easy for them to establish energy consumption targets. It also enables users to track their progress towards these goals, allowing for a better understanding of their energy consumption patterns. To incentivize sustainable behavior, Determined Green incorporates a point system that rewards users for their efforts, potentially leading to discounts on energy bills or other benefits.

By joining community challenges, users can actively participate in collective efforts to reduce energy consumption and carbon emissions. Users can compare their data to that of average people in their area as well as those living in homes similar to theirs. This comparison helps users gain insights into their energy consumption habits and motivates them to make changes.

Moreover, users have the opportunity to receive feedback on unusual energy usage, enabling them to identify and address potential inefficiencies. Device and room-specific consumption monitoring features further enhance users' ability to manage their energy usage effectively. Additionally, the app enables users to remotely control devices and schedule activities such as lighting to limit energy usage and promote energy efficiency.
