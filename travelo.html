Travelo Travel Company

The goal of this project was to design social media graphics for a travel company with a millennial demographic. The design was inspired by travel journals and blends the digital media with elements of the handmade and physical. This project includes two sets of nine social posts.


Scope
Branding
Social Media Graphics
Tools
Procreate
Photoshop
Illustrator
Timeframe
September 2021

Travelo is a travel company that caters specifically to millennial friend groups, offering an array of glamorous and trendy destinations around the globe. Their goal is to provide experiences that are not only visually appealing but also perfect for sharing on Instagram. Some of their featured destinations include British Columbia, Manchu Pichu, Bora Bora, Paris, Santorini, and Kyoto.

The branding is inspired by the concept of travel journaling. Social posts feature hand-drawn illustrations of different locations, showcasing their unique and highlighted features. The social background map imagery with place markers as references, giving travelers a sense of the various destination options Travelo provides around the globe. To evoke a sense of nostalgia and capture the moment, the images feature polaroid frames with washi tape-like shapes, mimicking the appearance of photographs pasted in a journal. The colors used in their design are soft and approachable, reflecting the natural world and harmonizing well with the imagery of the various destinations.

A set of nine Instagram posts were designed to highlight the single travel destination of Paris, France. Each post highlighted a different tourist attraction within the city. To maintain the journaling theme, the posts incorporated arrows and additional hand-drawn illustrations. Furthermore, a background map of France creates a cohesive element throughout the series of posts and highlights the location of the city with a map marker.
