Staying At Home

This book was designed using text gathered from Appalachian State University's Spring 2020 Intro to Graphic Design class from early on during the 2020 COVID-19 Pandemic. The design was meant to explore a more hopeful and positive perspective on the pandemic despite all the disruptions and difficulties associated with the quarantine experience.
