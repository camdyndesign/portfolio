Selections from the 2nd Floor

Branding proposal exploring a playful and fun approach to an exhibition. Inspired by ideas of celebration and breaking the traditional exhibition mold.


Scope
Branding
Tools
Illustrator
Photoshop
Timeframe
February 2020

Selections from the 2nd Floor is an exhibition of work from Appalachian State University's Senior Graphic Design Students. One of the goals of this project was to create an exhibition branding that not only felt cohesive with and representative of the featured design work but also created a gallery environment that was more approachable and immersive.

Exhibition Takeaways

To leave a memorable impression and encourage movement throughout the exhibition, the idea of a takeaway booklet was presented. Each page could be collected at individual projects to create a complete exhibition summary. The booklet was designed based on a color swatch fan deck as a way of representing the field of Graphic Design. Each page features individual projects and was RISO printed in one of the exhibition brand colors. The variety in color between each project allows for more color and play within the exhibition space. Stickers also served as a fun collectable and featured images of each work with a similar image treatment to RISO printing.

Floor and Wall Vinyl

A primary objective of this exhibition is to breakaway from the standard art on the walls. A way of achieving this is to engage the floor space through vinyl. The logo in vinyl on the floor helps introduce the exhibition space as guests enter the gallery. Hand drawn arrows and stars cover the floor and create movement throughout the space.
